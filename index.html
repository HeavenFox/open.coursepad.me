About CoursePad.me

Originally written for Cornell University, CoursePad.me is an easy-to-use course planning web application, with more than 12,000 users in the university.

CoursePad.me is designed with the explicit goal of low maintenance cost, user-friendly interface, and deliberate simplicity.

Licensed under Apache 2.0, a permissive open source license.

Get your Own CoursePad.me

Whether you plan to poke around the codebase, adapt it for your school, or improve the project, feel free to fork CoursePad.me on GitHub.
